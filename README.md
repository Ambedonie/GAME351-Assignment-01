# GAME351-Assignment-01
Group Assignment 1 for GAME351, Game Design in Unity

Controls: WASD, mouselook
Requires: Probuilder, Jammo (Robot Player), various tree prefabs(?)

It is on a strange, green planet that the Construct finds itself.
The emergency cockpit separation mechanism activated just before the rest of its ship crumpled against the unforgiving crust of this world's surface,
saving the Construct in the process. Its ship now little more than a smoldering heap of scrap, all that can be done is to tread the path ahead.
A strange building lies in wait--a sign of civilization. Hope--if the Construct's ilk could truly feel such a thing.
